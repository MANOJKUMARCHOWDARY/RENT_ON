How it works?

Step by step guide for renting a product from Rent-On-Need

  • Rent In
  • Rent Out

Book a product to rent

Search or directly browse through our categories for baby products, electronics, event rentals or medical equipment.

  • Click and open product details page
  • Select quantity and dates and add to cart
  • Check the details and checkout
  • Fill in delivery details and place the order

Receive the rented product

We will deliver the rented product to the address specified by you be it your home or office or an event venue. Please note that the payment of rent and deposit is upfront through online payment or cash on delivery

While we do the quality check at our end, you are also encouraged to check the product condition at the time of accepting delivery. This is highly recommended for electronics like laptops, projectors and LED TVs for rent.

Rental policy

Return the product

At the end period of the rented product, you will get an intimation to either extend or return the rented product, you can choose based on your need. Rent-On-Need team will arrange for pickup and deposit return. At the time of return:

  • Ensure the product is in good working condition
  • If there is any fault, please inform our team upfront
  • Write a review of your experience with Rent-On-Need
Tell your friends

Upload your product

We encourage business vendors to upload laptops, projectors and costumes on rent and individuals to upload baby products, home appliances and apparels to be rented out through Rent-On-Need

  • Select if you are a business or individual
  • Fill up a simple form with your details
  • Fill in the product details including rent
  • Hit submit to upload the product
Upload your product

Handover your product

Our Team will get in touch for validation, after which we will publish or make the product live on the website. Depending on the demand for the product people will book online.

Once an order is received in the system, you will get an email and/or a call from our operations team to keep the product ready. Handover the product for duration of the order plus one day each for delivery and pickup.

Get back your product

At the end of the period, we will return the product to you. Please ensure that product is in good working condition when returned, if there is any issue, report to operations team as soon as possible but within 24 hours of receiving product

  • Your share of rent will be settled monthly
  • If there is any fault, please inform within 24 hours
  • Refer Rent-On-Need to your friends for their rental requirements
