FAQ:

What is RSMiles?

It is our loyalty program where 1 RSMile = 1 INR.

How do I earn the miles?

You can earn RSmiles upon sign-up, through referrals or by renting.

Where do I use them?

You can use to rent anything, IT Equipment's - Laptops, Desktops, Server Systems or Audio Visual Equipment's

How much can I earn?

There is no limit to RSMiles you can earn.

Do the miles expire?

RSMiles expire as per policy. You can check expiry dates at https://www.rentsher.com/rsmiles
